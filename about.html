Events & Activations

Adventure MobileCade provides scalable gaming setups for a wide range of events across Platte City and the Kansas City metro. Below are our core event types and upcoming appearances.

Event Types

  • Community Festivals: Outdoor-friendly stations with retro and modern games.
  • Library Programs: Educational gaming, retro showcases, and youth engagement.
  • Apartment Complex Activations: Resident engagement with plug-and-play stations.
  • Private Parties: Birthdays, celebrations, and themed gaming nights.
  • Pop-Up Retail: Collectibles, retro cartridges, and impulse-buy displays.

Upcoming Events
